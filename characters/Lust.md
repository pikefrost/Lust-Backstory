Weaknesses: obsession candy.
Motivation: Master her vampire powers
Fears: none.

---

Origin Story

Lust was born into a forgotten vampire bloodline known for their supernatural hunting abilities. Unlike other vampires who embraced darkness, Lust learned to control her powers and use it as a weapon against powerful enemies.

After discovering her unique ability to sense weaknesses in opponents, she trained as a Hunter and developed a fighting style based on precision, movement, and unpredictable attacks. She created enchanted candy weapons that combine vampire magic with hunter techniques.

Her journey began when her clan was destroyed by a mysterious enemy. Now Lust travels across realms searching for the truth, hunting monsters, rival vampires.

---

Combat Style

Fighting Style:
Long-range precision combined with fast aerial Lollypop attacks.

Battle Strategy:
Lust keeps enemies at a distance with enchanted projectiles, waits for openings, then rushes in with powerful aerial combos using her giant Lollypop weapon.

Signature Weapon:
Enchanted Lollypop Arsenal — A magical weapon set that changes between a crossbow and a massive melee weapon.
